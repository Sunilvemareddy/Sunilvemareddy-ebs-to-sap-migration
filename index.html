Executive Summary

This master plan outlines the end-to-end approach to migrate an acquired Oracle EBS footprint into the corporate SAP environment. It covers program governance, data strategy, integration, testing, cutover, and post-go-live stabilisation.

Migration Methodology (Phases)

  • Prepare & Plan — Charter, scope, steering committee
  • Discover & Assess — inventory, customisation catalogue
  • Design — fit-gap, data mapping, interface design
  • Build — SAP config, ETL, interfaces
  • Test — multiple trial loads, UAT
  • Cutover — delta capture, final load, go-live
  • Stabilise — hypercare & PIR

Data Assessment & Mapping

Key activities: data inventory & profiling, cleansing, field-level mapping, ETL staging design, validation & reconciliation. Maintain data owner sign-offs for major master datasets.

Integration & Technical Architecture

Design integration layer (middleware or file-based), secure APIs, RBAC migration, and sizing for load performance. Keep a full interface inventory.

Testing & Validation

Perform unit, integration, E2E, performance, and reconciliation testing. Conduct multiple data migration mock runs and capture lessons per trial.

Cutover & Go-Live

Cutover runbook, freeze window, final extracts & delta loads, validation checkpoints, rollback criteria, communications plan, and hypercare teams.
