Communication Plan (High level)

Purpose: Keep stakeholders informed, align decisions, and manage expectations.

Stakeholders & channels

  • Steering Committee — Monthly meeting + pack (email)
  • Workstream Leads — Weekly status calls + Jira backlog

Frequency & content

  • Monthly: steering deck (executive summary + key decisions)
  • Pre go-live: daily communications during cutover week
