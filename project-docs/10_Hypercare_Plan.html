Hypercare Plan

Duration: Suggested: 4 weeks post go-live (adjustable).

Support model

  • Tier 2: Workstream SMEs (functional / data)

Activities

  • Daily stand-ups
  • Defect triage & priority fix
  • Knowledge transfer sessions
  • Stabilisation & handover to BAU
